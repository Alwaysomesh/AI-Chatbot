Introducing NeerMitra, a dedicated AI chatbot designed to address the pressing issue of water pollution in India. NeerMitra is a comprehensive and reliable source of information, offering insights on the state of water pollution and practical solutions to prevent its adverse effects. This intelligent chatbot has been meticulously trained on a wealth of data from authoritative sources, including the Ministry of Water Resources, Borgen Magazine, World Bank, Kaggle, and other reputable outlets. NeerMitra's mission is to empower individuals and communities with knowledge about water pollution and guide them towards meaningful actions to safeguard one of our most precious natural resources.
